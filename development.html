We have two main production plants, one in Roseville, Michigan and one in Chesterfield, Michigan. Since all of our products are IQF (individually quick-frozen), we must prepare the pasta, cook it, freeze it, and finally package it. We start by bringing the raw materials into the plant. All through production, our QC's take measurements and log information into a spreadsheet to ensure everything is going smoothly. When the product is finished, it's placed in a cardboard tote lined with packaging material. The totes are usually placed on top of a special palate jack that weighs the tote to ensure the weight matches quota. When a tote is finished, a QC logs the time at which the tote was finished.
