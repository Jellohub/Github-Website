What do we make?

We are an Italian company based in southern Michigan. We make a variety of pre-cooked products, including different kinds of rice, ravioli, linguini nests, farfalle, and rigatoni. They come frozen and are ready to eat in a couple minutes.

How do we make it?

We have two production plants, one based in Roseville and one based in Chesterfield. We combine flour with other ingredients such as water, salt and preservatives. We have people at each step of the way doing quality checks and making sure everything is going smoothly.

Who are our customers?

We have many customers. One of our biggest is ConAgra.
